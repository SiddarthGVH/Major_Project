# PULSE-CRM
"KALNET PULSE — Enterprise CRM with AI-based lead scoring, recommendations, and conversation intelligence"
1.1 What PULSE Is

PULSE is an enterprise Sales and Marketing CRM platform designed to manage the complete revenue lifecycle—from the first customer interaction to the successful closure of a deal. Rather than being just another contact management tool, PULSE provides a unified workspace where sales teams can manage leads, companies, contacts, deals, activities, and customer communication from a single platform.
